# Steam Girl Website - Image Requirements

## 🖼 Required Character Images

### Image Specifications
- **Format**: JPG or PNG (JPG preferred for photographs)
- **Dimensions**: 800x800px minimum (1:1 aspect ratio)
- **Quality**: High resolution, crisp details
- **File Size**: Under 2MB each for optimal loading
- **Style**: Pixar-inspired 3D rendering

## 🎨 Style Guidelines

### Color Palette to Use
- **Fire Elements**: #FF8C42 (sunset orange), #FF6B6B (coral pink)
- **Water Elements**: #4DA8DA (teal blue), #95E1D3 (mint green)
- **Steam Effects**: #FFE4C4 (soft cream), #F0F8FF (alice blue)
- **Skin Tone**: Warm, natural tones that work with magical lighting
- **Hair**: Any color that complements the magical theme

### Lighting Style
- Soft, warm lighting reminiscent of Pixar films
- Magical glow effects from the elements
- Avoid harsh shadows
- Create depth with subtle rim lighting
- Steam should have translucent, glowing quality

### Background Suggestions
- Soft gradient backgrounds
- Subtle magical particles
- Blurred elemental environments
- Keep backgrounds simple to focus on character
